# When can you start?

**Target time:** 10–15 seconds

---

## Talk track

> After offer acceptance, realistically **[date = notice end + buffer week]** — I want a clean handoff at my current role.
>
> If there's urgency on your side, I can discuss **buyout or early release** with my manager once we're aligned on offer.

---

## If available immediately

> I can start **within 1–2 weeks** of offer — paperwork and a short break, then full focus.

---

## Avoid

- "Tomorrow" if you're employed — credibility
- Vague "soon" — give a date or week
