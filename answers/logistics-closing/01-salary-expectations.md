# What are your salary expectations?

**Target time:** 15–20 seconds — customize before interview

---

## Talk track

> I'm flexible based on **total comp, role scope, and equity** — for a Senior Full Stack role at a startup at this scale I'm targeting roughly **[₹X–₹Y LPA]** / **[$X–$Y]** depending on how you structure base vs ESOP.
>
> Happy to align with your band if we're close on **level and responsibilities** — what's most important to me is the team and the work.

---

## Prep checklist

- [ ] Research the company / similar stage startup bands (Levels.fyi, Glassdoor, peers)
- [ ] Decide your **floor** (walk-away) vs **target** vs **stretch**
- [ ] Know if offer is INR, USD, remote India contract, etc.

---

## Avoid

- First number without a range — anchors low
- "Whatever you pay" — sounds unprepared
- Negotiating hard in round 2 technical — save for HR/offer stage unless they push
