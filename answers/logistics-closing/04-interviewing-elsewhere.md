# Are you interviewing elsewhere?

**Target time:** 15 seconds — honest, not desperate

---

## Talk track

> I'm **actively exploring** a few opportunities that match full stack / product engineering — **[optional: early stage / fintech / etc.]**.
>
> **This company is a top choice** because of the insurance platform problem and the stack fit. I'm not rushing the first offer — I want the right team — but I am moving with intent in the next **[few weeks]**.

---

## If only this company

> This company is my **primary focus** right now — the role and domain align well with what I want next.

---

## Avoid

- "I have five offers tomorrow" — pressure tactic backfires
- "You're my only option" — weak leverage
