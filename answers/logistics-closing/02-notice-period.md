# What is your notice period?

**Target time:** 10 seconds — **fill in your reality**

---

## Talk track

> My notice period is **[X weeks / X months]** with my current employer. I'd serve it professionally and hand off cleanly unless we mutually agree on an earlier release.

---

## If currently between jobs / freelancing

> I'm **not employed right now** / on **notice already** — I can start per your timeline after offer.

---

## Avoid

- Lying about notice — background checks bite
- Bad-mouthing current employer when explaining delay
