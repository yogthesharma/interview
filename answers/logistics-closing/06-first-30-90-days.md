# What would you want to work on in your first 30 / 90 days?

**Target time:** 45 seconds

---

## Talk track

> **First 30 days — learn and ship small:**
> - Onboard on **repo, CI, staging**, one service end-to-end  
> - Trace an **EOI or workflow** request through the platform — matches how I learn  
> - Pick up a **well-scoped ticket** — bug or small feature — merge with review, deploy  
> - Meet squad, understand **on-call and logging** conventions
>
> **By 90 days — own a slice:**
> - Own a **feature or service area** with minimal hand-holding  
> - Contribute to **code review** on React/TS/Node patterns  
> - Propose one **improvement** from pain I felt onboarding — docs, test gap, DX  
> - Comfortable debugging **cross-service** issues with request IDs / traces
>
> I'm not trying to rewrite the platform in week two — **earn trust, then expand**.

---

## Avoid

- "Fix everything" arrogance
- No concrete learning plan
