# What do you know about the insurance buying process?

**Target time:** 30–45 seconds

---

## Talk track

> At a high level — I'm not an insurance veteran, but I've researched how Typical fits in:
>
> **Group / employer benefits:** employee gets offered coverage through work → often goes through a **BenAdmin portal** (Workday, bswift) → may need to prove they're **insurable** (EOI) for voluntary life/disability above guaranteed limits → carrier **underwrites** → decision flows back → enrollment completes.
>
> **Pain today:** paper forms, weeks of back-and-forth, re-entering the same data, opaque status. **Digital flow:** SSO, pre-filled apps, instant decisions where rules allow.
>
> Parallels I know from other domains: **long workflows, compliance, async document generation** — same engineering muscles as visa flows at Atlys or campaign workflows at IQM.

---

## Avoid

- Claiming you know carrier actuarial math
- Treating all insurance as "click buy like Amazon"
