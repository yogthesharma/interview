# What is underwriting? (high level)

**Target time:** 20–30 seconds

---

## Talk track

> **Underwriting** is how an insurer decides **whether to offer coverage, at what price, and with what limits** — based on risk.
>
> They look at health history, age, coverage amount, sometimes occupation — and apply **rules or models** to approve, decline, rate up, or request more info.
>
> **Manual underwriting** = human reviewer. **Auto-adjudication** = rules engine decides instantly for straightforward cases — huge for UX when you can do it safely.
>
> **Group underwriting** (Platform expansion) = decisions at **employer/census** level — bulk employee data, not one person at a time.
