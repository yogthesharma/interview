# What is EOI? (if you've researched the company)

**Target time:** 30 seconds (see also `opening/06`)

---

## Talk track

> **EOI = Evidence of Insurability** — the workflow where an employee applying for **voluntary group benefits** (life, disability, etc.) provides health and eligibility info so the carrier can decide if they're coverable and at what rate.
>
> Not every enrollment needs EOI — **guaranteed issue** covers small amounts without questions. Above that limit, EOI kicks in.
>
> **The product angle:** digitize that — SSO from BenAdmin, **pre-filled** employee data, digital health questions, **auto-adjudication** where possible, sync decision back to employer systems. Replace paper and phone tag with something employees can finish in minutes.

---

## One-liner backup

> EOI is the "prove you're eligible for this coverage" step before the carrier says yes, no, or yes with conditions.

---

## Avoid

- Confusing EOI with the entire insurance purchase — it's the **insurability** step
