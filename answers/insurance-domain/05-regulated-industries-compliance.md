# Experience with regulated industries or compliance?

**Target time:** 30 seconds — honest

---

## Talk track

> I haven't worked **directly in insurance or HIPAA-certified systems**, but I've shipped in environments where **data sensitivity and correctness** mattered:
>
> - **Atlys** — visa/travel docs: PII, document handling, audit expectations from ops  
> - **IQM** — B2B ad platform: client data, billing accuracy, access control  
> - **General habits:** least-privilege auth, no PII in logs, env-based secrets, careful third-party integrations
>
> I understand regulated work means **slower intentional changes**, documentation, and asking before cutting corners on security. I'd ramp on The company's specific compliance (carrier requirements, health data policies) with the team — not pretend I'm already an expert.

---

## Avoid

- Claiming HIPAA audit experience if you don't have it
- Dismissing compliance as "blockers"
