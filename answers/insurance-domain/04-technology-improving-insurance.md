# How would technology improve insurance for customers?

**Target time:** 30–45 seconds

---

## Talk track

> **Speed and clarity** — people shouldn't wait weeks or re-type the same info. SSO + pre-fill + instant decisions where rules allow.
>
> **Transparency** — status tracking, clear next steps, fewer "call us" dead ends.
>
> **Accuracy** — validated forms, fewer rejected apps because of typos or missing fields.
>
> **Scale for carriers** — digital pipelines handle volume without linear headcount; group underwriting from census files instead of manual spreadsheets.
>
> **Trust** — secure handling of health data, audit trails, consistent decisions — tech has to earn that in a regulated space.
>
> That's why This domain resonates — it's not cosmetic; it's **workflow + platform** engineering.

---

## Avoid

- "Just make an app" without workflow/compliance awareness
