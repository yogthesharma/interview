# What are you looking for in your next team?

**Target time:** 30 seconds

---

## Talk track

> **Strong IC peers** — people I learn from in code review, especially on platform and AWS patterns.
>
> **Product-minded** — engineers who care about users and workflows, not only abstractions.
>
> **Healthy candor** — direct feedback, low politics, shared blame on incidents.
>
> **Room to own** — meaningful scope for a senior full stack person, not ticket factory.
>
> This role checks those boxes from what I've seen — insurance domain, event-driven platform, startup pace.

---

## Avoid

- Only listing perks (WFH, salary)
- "No meetings" — unrealistic
