# How do you prioritize when everything is urgent?

**Target time:** 30 seconds

---

## Talk track

> **User/business impact first** — what's blocking revenue, customers, or a launch date?
>
> **Separate urgent vs important** — prod down beats nice-to-have UI polish.
>
> **Communicate tradeoffs** — "If I take X, Y slips until Thursday" — let PM/lead decide with data.
>
> **Time-box** — don't let one rabbit hole eat the sprint; park deep refactors unless they're the fire.
>
> At IQM during campaign season everything felt P0 — we used **impact + deadline** in standup to pick the real top one or two.

---

## Avoid

- "I work harder" without a framework
- Doing everything mediocre
