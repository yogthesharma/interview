# Why a startup vs big company?

**Target time:** 30 seconds

---

## Talk track

> I've worked **startup-shaped teams** — Atlys (~100 when I joined), IQM product squads — not FAANG scale.
>
> **Why startup now:** I want **end-to-end ownership** — own a feature from API to UI to prod, see users impact quickly, influence architecture without six layers of approval. A company at ~50–100 engineers is that sweet spot: real platform problems, still small enough to matter individually.
>
> **Big co tradeoff I'd miss:** deep specialization and huge infra budgets. **What I gain:** speed, breadth, closer to product and customers — that's where I've been happiest and most effective.

---

## Avoid

- Bashing big companies
- "I can't handle structure" — sounds immature
