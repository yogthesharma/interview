# What kind of manager do you work best with?

**Target time:** 20–30 seconds

---

## Talk track

> Someone who gives **clear priorities and context** — why this matters for customers — then **trusts me to execute**.
>
> Available for **unblocking** (access, cross-team, scope fights), not micromanaging how I type every line.
>
> **Honest feedback** — tells me early if I'm off track; celebrates wins without fluff.
>
> I've had good managers who were former engineers — they understood tradeoffs when I pushed back on timelines or debt.

---

## Avoid

- "I don't need a manager"
- Complaining about past managers by name
