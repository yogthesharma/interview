# What does ownership mean to you?

**Target time:** 30 seconds

---

## Talk track

> **Ownership** means you're accountable for the **outcome**, not just your PR merging.
>
> - If prod breaks on your area, you **show up** — debug, fix, communicate  
> - You **think ahead** — edge cases, monitoring, docs for the next person  
> - You **partner with PM/design** — don't throw code over the wall  
> - You **don't hide behind "not my job"** when the user is blocked
>
> At Atlys owning visa flows, that meant frontend bugs *and* API contract issues *and* coordinating with ops when policies changed. That's the level I want on the team.

---

## Avoid

- "I only own my tickets"
- Heroics without collaboration
