# How do you balance speed vs quality?

**Target time:** 30 seconds

---

## Talk track

> **Default: sustainable speed** — tests and types on critical paths, code review, staging check. That's faster over a quarter than shipping bugs.
>
> **Know when to cut** — prototype or one-off script? lighter bar. **Money path / PII / auth?** higher bar, no shortcuts.
>
> **Scope over sloppiness** — ship a **thin vertical slice** well rather than a half-broken mega feature.
>
> Startups need velocity; **quality is what lets you keep velocity** after month six.

---

## Avoid

- Perfectionism that blocks launches
- "Move fast break things" on compliance-sensitive insurance data
