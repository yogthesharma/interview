# How do you stay updated with tech?

**Target time:** 20–30 seconds

---

## Talk track

> **Learning by building** — side projects (Boson, Zyvia) force me into Rust, SQLite, new React patterns.
>
> **Work** — migrations at IQM (React Query, TS strictness) kept me current on production patterns, not just tutorials.
>
> **Light intake** — Twitter/X eng accounts, occasional blog posts (Kent C. Dodds, etc.), docs for tools we use.
>
> I don't chase every new framework weekly — I **deepen** on stack that ships: React, TS, Node, AWS serverless concepts for this role.
