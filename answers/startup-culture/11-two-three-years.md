# Where do you see yourself in 2–3 years?

**Target time:** 30 seconds

---

## Talk track

> **Deep senior IC** on a platform at this scale — someone the team trusts on **hard full-stack problems**, event-driven workflows, and mentoring through reviews and pairing — not necessarily people management.
>
> I'd want to have **shipped meaningful product** — EOI or group underwriting modules customers rely on — and grown on **AWS serverless at scale**.
>
> Open to **tech lead path** if that's how ownership evolves, but my goal is **impact and craft**, not title chasing.
