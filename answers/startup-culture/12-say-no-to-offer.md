# What would make you say no to an offer?

**Target time:** 20–30 seconds — diplomatic

---

## Talk track

> I'd decline if fundamentals didn't line up:
>
> - **Role mismatch** — mostly maintenance with no ownership, or pure frontend when the job is full stack platform  
> - **Culture red flags** — blame-heavy, no psychological safety, unrealistic permanent crunch  
> - **Comp / level** far apart with no path to fix  
> - **Ethics** — building something I couldn't stand behind
>
> I'm excited about the company specifically, so this is hypothetical — I'd rather discuss **what would make me say yes long-term**: growth, impact, team fit.

---

## Avoid

- Listing company-specific negatives
- "Only money" — sounds mercenary
