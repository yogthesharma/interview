# What are you hoping to get out of this conversation today?

**Target time:** 20–30 seconds  
**Format:** Show intent — mutual evaluation, not just "I want the job"

---

## Talk track

> Mainly three things.
>
> **First**, I'd like to show how I think on **real engineering problems** — whether that's coding, system discussion, or walking through something I've built — since that's what the day-to-day work is.
>
> **Second**, I want to understand **how your team builds** — especially platform patterns, code review culture, and what "good" looks like for a senior full-stack engineer here. Shubham's work on scaling services and performance is actually one of the reasons I was excited for this round.
>
> **Third**, I want to leave with an honest sense of **fit** — whether my background in React, Node, and owning product workflows maps to what you need on the team right now.

---

## Shorter version

> I want to demonstrate how I solve problems and learn whether this team and the technical challenges are where I can contribute most over the next few years.

---

## Avoid

- "I just want to pass" — low energy
- Only asking about WFH / perks
- Making it one-sided ("I hope you see I'm great") — frame as mutual fit
