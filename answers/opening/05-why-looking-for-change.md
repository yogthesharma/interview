# Why are you looking for a change?

**Target time:** 20–30 seconds  
**Format:** Forward-looking, never badmouth past employers

---

## Talk track (adjust to your real situation)

> I left **Atlys** in mid 2025 after a focused stint on their visa application platform. Since then I've deliberately spent time **building my own products** — Zyvia and **Boson** (open source, Rust + React) — to stay sharp on full-stack ownership and ship 0→1 products.
>
> Now I'm looking to bring that energy back into a **strong product team** where I can own meaningful features at scale long term. Typical fits because it's full-stack, platform-heavy, and at a stage where individual ownership really moves the needle.

---

## Alternative angles (pick what's honest)

**If they ask why you left Atlys specifically:**
> Good stint — shipped a lot on visa workflows — but I wanted a period to build independently and level up on **Rust/systems** and product architecture. Ready for the next long-term team.

**If they ask about gap / personal projects:**
> Not a gap — shipped two products and an npm library. Chose depth and ownership over rushing to the next logo.

---

## Avoid

- Negativity about Atlys, IQM, or managers
- "I was bored" — reframe as growth
- Money as the first reason
