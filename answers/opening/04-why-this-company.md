# Why this company?

**Target time:** 30–45 seconds  
**Format:** Genuine + connected to your skills and their stack

---

## Talk track

> Three reasons.
>
> **First, the problem** — insurance still has painful, slow buying experiences. The company is fixing that with modern product and engineering, and that kind of real-world impact matters to me more than incremental UI tweaks.
>
> **Second, the engineering** — you're building a **scalable, event-driven platform** on AWS — Lambda, DynamoDB, many services. I've done full-stack product work with APIs, auth, and performance at scale; Boson taught me **config-driven workflows and local secret discipline**; I want to apply that mindset on a **platform like yours**.
>
> **Third, the stage and role** — startup-shaped: ownership, greenfield modules, multiple hats. That's where I've done my best work — Atlys owning visa flows, IQM growing frontend into backend. A Sr Full Stack role here is exactly that responsibility.

---

## Research hooks (mention naturally if true)

- EOI / group underwriting platform
- Event-driven workflows (EventBridge)
- Multi-tenant B2B SaaS
- Config-validated before execution (parallel to Boson `lint` / `doctor`)

- Generic "I love startups"
