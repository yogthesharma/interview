# What's the size of your current team?

**Target time:** 15–25 seconds

---

## Talk track

> **Right now** it's just me on Boson — I'm the entire engineering team for the product.
>
> **Before that**, at **Atlys** I was on a **small product squad** — engineers, product, and design working closely on visa flows. I don't remember the exact headcount, but it wasn't a large platform org — you knew everyone on the feature.
>
> At **IQM**, similar — **small frontend/full-stack group** supporting multiple internal products. I wasn't a lead, but within that squad I was one of the more senior ICs doing reviews and running ceremonies.

---

## Framing note

Honest about solo now; for past roles use **"small squad"** if exact numbers are fuzzy. Don't invent "team of 12."

---

## If they ask "how did you work without a lead?"

> We had a manager and senior folks, but day-to-day the squad self-organized. I often **facilitated standups and sprint syncs** and **reviewed PRs** — leadership without the title.
