# Have you worked in a startup environment before?

**Target time:** 20–30 seconds

---

## Talk track

> Yes — in different flavors.
>
> **Atlys** felt very startup-like: fast shipping, high ownership, visa product moving quickly, fewer layers between me and production.
>
> **IQM** was more established, but my squad moved fast — small team, direct access to product, lots of autonomy on features.
>
> And I've spent the last year **building my own products** — Zyvia and Boson — which is the ultimate startup mode: zero bureaucracy, you own everything.
>
> I enjoy environments where **you're accountable for outcomes**, not just tickets — that's why This kind of role appeals to me.

---

## Avoid

- Pretending IQM was a 5-person startup if it wasn't — "squad autonomy within a larger org" is honest
