# What's the biggest technical challenge right now?

**Why ask:** Shows interest; their answer hints what you'd work on.

---

## How to ask

> From your perspective, what's the **biggest technical challenge** the team is facing right now — scale, multi-tenant, legacy migration, observability, something else?

---

## Follow-up if natural

> Is that something a new senior engineer could **meaningfully contribute to** in the first few months?

---

## Listen for

- Event-driven complexity, DynamoDB modeling, PDF/email pipelines  
- Hiring reason behind the role
