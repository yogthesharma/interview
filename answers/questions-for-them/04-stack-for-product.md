# What's the stack for the product I'd work on?

**Why ask:** Confirm React/TS/Node/AWS alignment; shows you care about reality not JD boilerplate.

---

## How to ask

> For the product area I'd likely join — what's the **day-to-day stack**? React version, Node/Lambda patterns, data stores, event bus — anything that's non-obvious from the outside?

---

## Listen for

- Monorepo vs many repos  
- Testing frameworks  
- Feature flag / config tooling
