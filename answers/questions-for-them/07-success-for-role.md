# How do you measure success for this role?

**Why ask:** Clarifies what "good" looks like at 6 months.

---

## How to ask

> If we're having this conversation again in **six months** and you're glad you hired me — what would I have **delivered or demonstrated**?

---

## Listen for

- Shipping features vs platform reliability  
- Mentoring / review participation  
- Domain learning expectations
