# What does on-call look like?

**Why ask:** Honest lifestyle question; tie to your incident experience.

---

## How to ask

> With **many serverless services**, what does **on-call** look like for this team — rotation length, alert volume, how you handle cross-service incidents?

---

## Listen for

- PagerDuty vs business-hours  
- Runbooks, tracing maturity  
- Expectation for new hires on rotation timeline
