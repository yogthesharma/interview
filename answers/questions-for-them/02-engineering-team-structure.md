# How is the engineering team structured?

**Why ask:** Know your squad, platform vs product, who you'd report to.

---

## How to ask

> How is **engineering organized** — product squads vs platform, and where would this role sit? How big is the team I'd work with day to day?

---

## Listen for

- EOI vs group underwriting vs shared platform  
- Frontend/backend split or full-stack squads  
- India vs US distribution (if relevant)
