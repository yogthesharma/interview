# What does a typical day look like for this role?

**Why ask:** Surfaces meetings vs coding vs on-call — sets expectations.

---

## How to ask

> What does a **typical day or week** look like for someone in this Senior Full Stack role — split between feature work, reviews, incidents, and planning?

---

## Listen for

- Standup / sprint rhythm  
- Pairing vs solo  
- Customer or carrier-facing work  
- On-call load mixed into "typical day"
