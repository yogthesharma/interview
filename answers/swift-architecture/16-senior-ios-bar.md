# What makes a senior iOS/macOS engineer hirable — your bar?

**Target time:** 45–60 seconds

---

## Talk track

> **Senior bar (what I'd look for hiring):**
>
> - **Ships** reliable features — lifecycle, memory, threading not an afterthought
> - **Architecture judgment** — MVVM/coordinators/repos without over-engineering
> - **Production mind** — errors, logging, crashes, offline, accessibility
> - **Code review** — elevates others, catches footguns
> - **Pragmatism** — SwiftUI vs UIKit tradeoffs, brownfield empathy
> - **Communication** — clarifies requirements, estimates honestly
>
> **Not senior:** only tutorials, can't debug retain cycle, no opinion on testing strategy.
>
> **My lens:** ~5.5 years shipping full-stack product — I'd hold myself to same **ownership + production** bar on iOS even while deepening platform-specific craft.

---

## Green flags

- Explains tradeoffs, not buzzwords
- Stories with metrics (crash down, latency)
- Tests behavior that matters

---

## Avoid

- Equating senior with "knows every Apple API"
- Portfolio with no production constraints discussed
