# App Store review rejections you've handled or heard about?

**Target time:** 30–45 seconds

---

## Talk track

> Common **Guideline** rejections (awareness — not claiming personal history for all):
>
> - **2.1 Crashes / bugs** on review device — provide demo account, fix edge cases
> - **4.2 Minimum functionality** — web wrapper, too thin
> - **5.1 Privacy** — missing purpose strings, data collection mismatch with Privacy Nutrition Label
> - **3.1 Payments** — digital goods must use IAP
> - **Login** — must offer account deletion; Sign in with Apple if other social logins
> - **Background modes** — declared but unused
>
> **Response:** concise Resolution Center note, repro steps, video if needed, expedite only for critical fixes.
>
> **My angle:** haven't been primary App Store liaison — understand checklist from docs and peers; web deploys had similar compliance gates (cookies, PII).

---

## Avoid

- Arguing with reviewer without reproduction steps
- Hiding features from review with geo-fencing (ban risk)
